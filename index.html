Our Story

India's Streets,
Tampa's Heart

Dosthi Chowrastha is a pioneer in Indian food trucking in Tampa, FL. Loved by USF students and local families alike, we bring back memories of India's lively streets through irresistible dishes.

From our famous Irani chai to street-style hakka noodles, mirchi bhaji, crispy dosas, and paneer 65 — every bite is a sensory journey. Our space welcomes everyone — students, families, and food lovers creating memories over authentic cuisine.

Known for bringing the spirit of India’s lively chowrasthas to Tampa, Dosthi blends casual hangout energy with a menu that now stretches from chai and dosas to frankies, chaats, mandi, and more.
